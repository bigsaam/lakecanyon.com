Monthly Board Meetings

Your LCMWC Board holds monthly board meetings, usually on the third Wednesday of each month via Zoom video call. Please check your email for instructions on dialing in via Zoom.

All Lake Canyon property owners and residents are invited to attend.

Annual Spring Members Meeting — May 3, 2025

At the meeting, the LCMWC discussed several topics including:

  • Annual Financial Review, including the board's intention to raise rates
  • Year in Review: What LCMWC has accomplished since September 2024
  • Year Upcoming: LCMWC priorities for the rest of the year

This year's Spring Meeting included a short Lake Canyon Firewise Committee presentation, with information about an upcoming Emergency Communication Drill.

Consumer Confidence Report (CCR)

Our 2024 Consumer Confidence Report regarding water quality is now available. We test drinking water quality for many constituents as required by state and federal regulations.

This report shows the results of our monitoring for January 1 – December 31, 2024.
